Pattern 1: paraphrasing + summary in a paragraph

Para 3 注意事项

时态

默认用一般现在时（流程是客观事实，无时间变化）。

若流程有“过去步骤”（如历史工艺），用一般过去时。

语态

以被动语态为主。

强调“事物被操作”，而非“谁操作”。

The raw material is transported to the factory.

Workers transport the raw material...

除非题目明确提及“人”。

词汇替换

避免重复使用词汇。

被动语态定义

被动语态表示主语是动作的承受者，而不是动作的发出者。流程图写作中，我们通常关注“材料/产品经历了什么步骤”，所以常用被动语态。

基本结构： subject + be + past participle

流程图常用： sugar cane is harvested / the juice is filtered / the liquid is heated

如果题目强调机器、工人或农民，也可以补充 by + doer，但不要每一句都写。

The diagram illustrates how sugar is manufactured from sugar cane, a process that can be outlined into seven consecutive steps, beginning with the growing of sugar cane and ending with the drying and cooling of the final product.

Sugar cane is first cultivated over a period of 12 to 18 months before being harvested, either mechanically by machine or manually by workers. The harvested cane is then conveyed to a crushing facility, where it is pulverized to extract the juice. This juice is subsequently strained through a limestone filter to remove impurities and contaminants.

The purified liquid is then fed into an evaporator, where it is subjected to heat until transformed into a thick syrup. In the following stage, the syrup is processed in a centrifuge, which separates the sugar crystals from the remaining liquid. Finally, the extracted crystals undergo a drying and cooling process, yielding the refined sugar ready for consumption.

Causes / Solutions 逻辑链

Cause 1 — Mental Health / Perception

情境：司机在路上很少看到警察或摄像头

→ 直接效果：他们认为被抓的可能性很小

→ 对人的影响：觉得接电话或超速带来的方便比可能被罚更重要

→ 长期后果：越来越多的人不把交通规则当回事

Cause 2 — Personal Growth / Overconfidence

情境：司机过去超速或看手机时从未出事

→ 直接效果：他们觉得自己技术好，别人出事是因为别人笨

→ 对人的影响：对交通规则变得轻视和不在意

→ 长期后果：继续保持危险习惯，最终可能导致严重事故

Cause 3 — Efficiency & Productivity / Modern Life Pressure

情境：工作中要随时回消息、准时送到货或赶到会场

→ 直接效果：司机觉得交通规则耽误时间，挡了自己的路

→ 对人的影响：把安全和规则放在第二位，把效率放在第一位

→ 长期后果：危险驾驶变成普遍现象，大家习以为常

Solutions

Solution 1 — Enforcement

情境：路上增加警察巡逻和自动摄像头，罚款金额提高 → 违规减少，道路变得更安全

Solution 2 — Awareness

情境：驾校课程中加入真实事故视频，媒体播放安全宣传 → 安全驾驶成为社会习惯

Road Safety 范文挖空

当今时代, road safety has become 日益担忧的问题. There is 一个显著现象 that many motorists continue to 违反交通法规 by speeding or using mobile phones while driving, 尽管有规定. This essay will 分析原因 before suggesting 可行措施.

几个可能原因. First, 错误认知 that the chance of being punished is low is 主要因素, since when drivers rarely see police or cameras, they judge the convenience of speeding or phone use to 超过风险. For example, drivers in many busy cities regularly use phones at red lights since 巡逻不常见. Another essential reason is 过度自信态度 that ignores real dangers: having broken rules before without crashing, many drivers convince themselves they are 技术足够好.

Ultimately, it requires 共同努力 to address this challenge effectively. At the community level, 通过教育提高意识 is key, as it can 消除虚假自信 by showing them the real consequences of dangerous driving, as UK courses do with crash-survivor videos. Meanwhile, it is high time the authorities attempted to 加强执法 through frequent patrols, automated cameras, and 更严厉处罚 such as heavy fines or licence suspensions, as seen in Australia, where tough fines for phone use have 显著减少违规.

In conclusion, 虚假的安全感 and overconfidence are 主要原因. To resolve it successfully, the top priority is to 实施严格持续执法, while at the same time motivating people to 形成真正意识.
